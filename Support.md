## Getting Help

### Common Issues & Fixes

#### Camera Not Working
LabelAIScanner needs camera access to scan ingredient labels.

Try the following:
- Open **Settings** → **Privacy & Security** → **Camera**
- Make sure **LabelAIScanner** is enabled
- Close and reopen the app
- Restart your device if the issue continues

#### No Ingredients Detected
If no text is recognized:
- Make sure the label is well‑lit
- Hold the phone steady and keep the ingredient list centered
- Move closer to the label
- Avoid glare or reflections
- Clean your camera lens
- Try scanning from a different angle

#### Apple Intelligence Not Available
LabelAIScanner relies on Apple Intelligence for ingredient analysis.

Please check:
- Your device is running **iOS 26 or later**
- Apple Intelligence is available in your region
- Apple Intelligence is enabled in **Settings** → **Apple Intelligence & Siri**
- You have at least **8GB of free storage**

Restart your device after enabling Apple Intelligence.

#### Subscription Not Working
If Pro features aren’t unlocked:
- Make sure you’re signed in with your Apple ID
- Check your internet connection
- Close and reopen the app
- Restart your device

If the issue persists, contact support.

#### Free Trial Not Applied
- Free trials are applied automatically on your first subscription
- Trial length: **7 days**
- Your subscription renews automatically unless canceled
- You can cancel anytime in  
  **Settings** → **[Your Name]** → **Subscriptions** → **LabelAIScanner**

#### Low or No Scan Credits
- Free users receive **2 scans** to test the app
- Pro users get **unlimited scans**

## Tips for Best Results

### Better Scans
- Use bright, even lighting
- Avoid shadows or glare
- Keep the ingredient list fully visible
- Hold the phone steady

### Faster Analysis
- Ensure a stable internet connection
- Close other apps to free up memory
- Wait for analysis to complete before leaving the scan screen

## Troubleshooting

### App Crashes or Won’t Open
1. Force close the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update iOS to the latest version
4. Delete and reinstall LabelAIScanner

### Blank Camera Screen
- Check camera permissions in  
  **Settings** → **Privacy & Security** → **Camera**
- Enable LabelAIScanner
- Restart the app

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my data private?**  
Yes. All analysis happens on your device, and we never store your photos or
ingredient data.

**Can I use LabelAIScanner offline?**  
No. Apple Intelligence requires an internet connection for ingredient analysis.

**How accurate is ingredient detection?**  
Accuracy is over 95% on most standard labels. Results may vary with poor lighting,
damaged packaging, or unusual fonts.

**Can I share scan results?**  
Not yet, but sharing features are planned for a future update.

**What if an ingredient explanation is wrong?**  
Ingredient explanation is provided by apple intelligence, at the moment there's nothing we can do.

**Do you sell my data?**  
No. We never sell or share user data.
