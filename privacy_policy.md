# Privacy Policy for LabelAIScanner

Last updated: January 23, 2026

## Overview

LabelAIScanner is designed with a "privacy-first" philosophy. We believe that your data belongs to you, and we have built this application to ensure that your information remains private and secure on your device.

## No Data Collection

LabelAIScanner **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**. 
- We do not have accounts or user profiles.
- We do not collect usage statistics or analytics.
- We do not track your location or any other sensitive information.
- Any images scanned or text processed stays exclusively on your device.

## No Internet Access

LabelAIScanner **does not access the internet**.
- The application is fully functional offline.
- No data is ever sent to external servers or third-party services.
- There are no background processes that communicate with the web.

## Apple Intelligence & On-Device Processing

All artificial intelligence features in LabelAIScanner are powered by **Apple Intelligence**, Apple's own on-device Large Language Model (LLM) technology.
- All AI processing occurs locally on your iPhone or iPad.
- Your data never leaves your device for the purpose of AI analysis.
- We do not use any cloud-based AI services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our commitment to collecting no data and maintaining full on-device privacy will remain the core of LabelAIScanner.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to us through the App Store support channels.
